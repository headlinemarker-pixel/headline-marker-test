Upcoming Development Plans

Introduction: This extension is being developed by a coding beginner in collaboration with generative AI. While development may take time, we prioritize features that many users request. Thank you for your patience and support!

  • ・Sync Function：
    Sync your saved headings with your Google Account to access them across multiple devices.
  • ・Heading Categorization：
    Group headings by topic (e.g., A/B projects) and export/import for separate workspace management.
  • ・Heading Management Dashboard：
    Manage, search, and sort all saved headings in one place.
  • ・External Integration：
    Connect with apps like Evernote or Google Keep.
  • ・Memo Function：
    Add and display short notes linked to each heading.
  • ・UI / Billing Enhancements：
    Continuous improvements to user experience and payment management flow.

※ These are planned features and may not be implemented yet.

※ Priorities may change based on user feedback and usage trends.

※ The order shown does not represent the implementation order.
